Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are our top priorities

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At AudioX, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our audiobook platform and services.

Our Promise: We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely to provide and improve our services for you.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password
  • • Profile Data: Profile picture, preferences, language settings
  • • Contact Information: Email address for communications
  • • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through Stripe)

Usage Information

  • • Listening History: Audiobooks played, progress, bookmarks
  • • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser information
  • • App Usage: Features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • • Location Data: General location (country/city) for content recommendations

Technical Information

  • • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed
  • • Cookies: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies
  • • Performance Data: App crashes, loading times, error reports

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • • Provide access to audiobook content
  • • Maintain your listening progress
  • • Process payments and subscriptions
  • • Provide customer support
  • • Send service-related notifications

Personalization

  • • Recommend relevant audiobooks
  • • Customize your experience
  • • Remember your preferences
  • • Provide personalized content
  • • Improve our recommendation algorithms

Security & Safety

  • • Prevent fraud and abuse

Analytics & Improvement

  • • Analyze usage patterns
  • • Improve our services

  • • Optimize performance
  • • Conduct research and analytics

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT Sell Your Data

AudioX does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your privacy is not for sale.

Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform (payment processing, cloud hosting, analytics). These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety of our users.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

Data Security

Encryption & Protection

  • • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • • Encrypted storage of sensitive data
  • • Regular security audits and updates
  • • Secure payment processing via Stripe

Access Controls

  • • Limited employee access to user data
  • • Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
  • • Regular access reviews and monitoring
  • • Data minimization principles

Your Privacy Rights

You Have Control Over Your Data

We believe you should have control over your personal information. Here are the rights you have regarding your data:

Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal data we have about you.

Correct Your Data

Update or correct any inaccurate personal information.

Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements).

Data Portability

Export your data in a machine-readable format.

Opt-Out

Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

Restrict Processing

Limit how we use your personal information.

Cookies and Tracking

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a better user experience. We use both session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (remain until deleted or expired).

Essential Cookies

Required for basic functionality like login and security.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how you use our platform to improve it.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences for a better experience.

Children's Privacy

Age Restrictions

AudioX is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. Parents who believe their child has provided us with personal information should contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

AudioX is based in Pakistan, but we may use service providers located in other countries. When we transfer your personal information internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

Safeguards Include:

  • • Standard contractual clauses approved by data protection authorities
  • • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • • Account data: Until account deletion
  • • Listening history: 3 years after last activity
  • • Payment records: 7 years for tax purposes
  • • Support communications: 2 years

Deletion Process

When you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it for legal, regulatory, or security purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
