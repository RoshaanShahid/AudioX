Anti-Piracy Policy

Protecting intellectual property and supporting content creators

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

AudioX is committed to protecting the intellectual property rights of authors, publishers, and content creators. We have zero tolerance for piracy and unauthorized distribution of copyrighted content.

Our Mission: To create a sustainable ecosystem where creators are fairly compensated for their work, and users have access to high-quality, legally obtained audiobook content.

What Constitutes Piracy

Prohibited Activities

  • Unauthorized downloading, copying, or distribution of AudioX content
  • Sharing login credentials to access premium content
  • Recording, capturing, or reproducing audiobooks for redistribution
  • Uploading copyrighted content without proper authorization
  • Using automated tools to bypass payment systems

Our Protection Measures

Technical Safeguards

  • • Digital Rights Management (DRM) protection
  • • Encrypted audio streaming
  • • User authentication and access controls
  • • Download restrictions and time limits
  • • Watermarking technology

Monitoring Systems

  • • Automated piracy detection
  • • User behavior analysis
  • • Content fingerprinting
  • • Regular security audits
  • • Community reporting system

Account Actions

1
First Offense: Warning and temporary suspension
2

3

Legal Consequences

AudioX reserves the right to pursue legal action against individuals or organizations engaged in piracy. This may include civil lawsuits for damages and cooperation with law enforcement agencies.

DMCA Compliance

Digital Millennium Copyright Act

AudioX complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and responds promptly to valid takedown notices from copyright holders.

DMCA Notice Requirements:

  • • Identification of copyrighted work
  • • Location of infringing material
  • • Contact information of copyright holder
  • • Good faith statement of unauthorized use
  • • Statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury
  • • Physical or electronic signature

Supporting Content Creators

Fair Compensation

We ensure that authors, narrators, and publishers receive fair compensation for their work through our transparent revenue-sharing model.

Creator Protection

Our advanced protection systems safeguard creator content from unauthorized use while providing detailed analytics and reporting.
