About AudioX

Born from a vision to make literature accessible across Pakistan through the power of audio storytelling

Our Story

AudioX began as a Final Year Project (FYP) with a simple yet powerful mission

The Vision

We recognized a significant gap in the Pakistani digital content landscape. While audiobooks were gaining popularity worldwide, there was limited content available in local languages like Urdu, Punjabi, and Sindhi.

The Challenge

Our challenge was to create a platform that not only serves the English-speaking audience but also celebrates and promotes Pakistani literature and storytelling traditions in native languages.
