Meet Our Team

The passionate minds behind AudioX - your favorite audiobook platform

Our Story

AudioX was born from the collaborative effort of two passionate computer science students who shared a vision of making literature accessible to everyone in Pakistan. What started as a Final Year Project has grown into a platform that serves thousands of users across the country.

Our Mission: To bridge the gap between traditional literature and modern technology, making audiobooks accessible in multiple Pakistani languages while fostering a love for reading and learning.
